Applications

Four feed types. One platform.

greenLi technology adapts to the lithium source. Whether you operate a salar, a refinery, a geothermal plant, or a desalination facility, the same separation platform unlocks lithium you currently lose, ignore, or pay to dispose of.

Use Cases

Where the platform deploys.

Salar Brine

~2,000 ppm Li

For operators of: SQM, Albemarle, Allkem, Eramet, YPF, Rio Tinto-class operations.

  • ~39% OpEx reduction per m³ of feed
  • >19% capacity uplift on the same brine volume
  • De-lithiated effluent suitable for aquifer re-injection, supporting sustainable brine fracking
  • Wider profit margins on existing reserves

Available as: Separation as a service, priced per m³. Modular skids deployed on site. No disruption to existing precipitation lines.

Refinery Wastewater

>30,000 ppm Li

For operators of: Talison, Covalent, Core Lithium, Pilbara, and other spodumene refining operations.

  • Convert a waste stream into a revenue stream
  • Reduce environmental liability on tailings discharge
  • Improve overall lithium recovery rate without changing upstream operations
  • Industry-wide, only ~4% of this capacity is currently captured

Available as: Bolt-on membrane skid integrated with existing wastewater handling. Capture-and-transfer suite available from year three for on-site cathode precursor production.

Start a Refinery Pilot

Geothermal & Oilfield Brine

100–500 ppm Li

For operators of: Vulcan Energy, Cornish Lithium, Ormat, Fervo, PetroSun, oilfield "produced water" operators in the US Gulf states.

  • Add a revenue stream to existing energy operations
  • Process produced water at the wellhead, eliminating disposal logistics
  • Validate lithium reserves on assets you already control

Available as: Modular skid integration with existing brine handling infrastructure.

Seawater & Desalination

~0.17 ppm Li (seawater)

For operators of: IDE Technologies, TAQA, AcuaMed, and other large-scale desalination operations.

  • Co-product revenue from existing desalination operations
  • Reduced brine reject disposal costs
  • Strategic positioning in the lithium supply chain through infrastructure you already own

Available as: Full technology suite, deployed on the brine reject line. Pilot capacity targeted for 2029.
