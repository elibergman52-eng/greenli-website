Team

Built by engineers, scientists, and operators who have done this before.

greenLi is a spinout from Prof. Hadas Mamane's laboratory at Tel Aviv University, backed by Earth & Beyond Ventures and a syndicate of deeptech co-investors. The team brings together membrane science, electrochemistry, environmental engineering, IP law, and operational experience in the desalination industry.
