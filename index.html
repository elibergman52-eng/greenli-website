Lithium Extraction Technology

We extract lithium from water.

A proprietary membrane platform that separates lithium from any aqueous source — salar brine, refinery wastewater, geothermal fluid, seawater. Higher recovery, lower cost, fewer dependencies.

Explore the Technology

The lithium supply chain is brittle. Extraction efficiency sits between 25 and 50 percent. Brine evaporation takes 18 months. Spodumene rock leaves significant capacity unrecovered. The geographic concentration of refining capacity creates geopolitical exposure that EV manufacturers and battery producers can no longer absorb.

Demand is set to outpace supply for the next decade.
