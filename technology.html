Our Technologies

Complementary by design.

M.L.E.

Membrane Lithium Extraction

Using a proprietary Li-selective membrane developed at Tel Aviv University, our M.L.E. system separates Li from competing ions, purifying Li-containing aqueous resources at any concentration. Our system transposes the desalination industry into an effective, continuous D.L.E. solution.

+ Agnostic to lithium source
+ Modular and scalable
+ Continuous operation, no evaporation
+ Pilot-ready Q4 2026
Deep Dive: M.L.E. → TRL 4 → 6 · Q3 2026

D.C.M.

Direct Cathode Manufacture

With a revolutionary approach, our proprietary system offers a "brine-to-battery" solution that generates high quality battery-ready cathode powders directly from purified Li brines. With D.C.M., proprietors of lithium assets can vertically integrate down the supply chain.

+ Agnostic to cathode chemistry
+ Bolt-on to any DLE or mine
+ Vertical integration without a refinery
+ Pilot-ready Q2 2027
Deep Dive: D.C.M. → TRL 3 → 5 · Q3 2026
