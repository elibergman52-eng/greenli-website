Our Technologies

Complementary by design.

M.L.E.

Membrane Lithium Extraction

Using a proprietary Li-selective membrane developed at Tel Aviv University, our M.L.E. system separates Li from competing ions, purifying Li-containing aqueous resources at any concentration. Our system transposes the desalination industry into an effective, continuous D.L.E. solution.

+ Agnostic to Li source
+ Rapidly scalable
+ Modular deployment
+ Pilot-ready Q4 2026
Deep Dive: M.L.E. → TRL 4 → 6 · Q3 2026

D.C.M.

Direct Cathode Manufacture

With a revolutionary approach, our proprietary system offers a "brine-to-battery" solution that generates high quality battery-ready cathode powders directly from purified Li brines. With D.C.M., proprietors of lithium assets can vertically integrate down the supply chain.

+ Agnostic to cathode chemistry
+ Pilot-ready Q2 2027
+ Bolt-on to mines / DLE
+ Vertical integration
Deep Dive: D.C.M. → TRL 3 → 5 · Q3 2026
Intellectual Property

Three protected innovations.

M.L.E.

Li-selective Membrane

The only spiral-wound, lithium-selective separation product on the market outside of LG. Protected and ready for scale-up via established desalination manufacturing.

D.C.M.

Solid-phase Capture Material

Proprietary material concentrates lithium from the enriched permeate stream for direct cathode integration. No known direct competition in this step of the process.

D.C.M.

Cathode Transfer Process

Proprietary process transfers captured lithium directly onto cathode material on site. Eliminates intermediate transport and chemical conversion. No known direct competition.
