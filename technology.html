Technology

Membrane separation, built for lithium.

Three proprietary processes that work together: a lithium-selective membrane, a solid-phase capture material, and a transfer step that produces battery-grade cathode precursor on site.

How It Works

Three processes. One platform.

01

Separate

Lithium-selective membrane isolates lithium ions from a mixed aqueous feed. Deployed in a conventional spiral-wound configuration with proven scale-up paths from desalination. The output is a high-purity, lithium-enriched permeate that replaces multi-stage chemical purification.

Only spiral-wound Li-selective membrane outside of LG
02

Capture

Lithium ions are captured on a proprietary solid material. This step concentrates lithium for downstream conversion and decouples extraction throughput from cathode production timing.

03

Transfer

A proprietary electrochemical process transfers captured lithium directly onto cathode material. Eliminates the conventional chemical conversion stage, removes intermediate transport, and enables on-site cathode manufacture.

No known direct competition on capture + transfer steps
